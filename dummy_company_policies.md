# Acme Corp — Company Policies & Employee Handbook

Welcome to the Acme Corp Employee Handbook. This document outlines the core operational policies, guidelines, and benefits for all team members. These policies apply to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees.

---

## 1. Core Values & Workplace Conduct

### 1.1 Mutual Respect
Acme Corp is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone. We do not tolerate discrimination or harassment of any kind based on race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or age.

### 1.2 Communication Guidelines
*   **Transparency:** We communicate openly and encourage constructive feedback.
*   **Professionalism:** Maintain a professional tone in all public and internal communications, including Slack channels, email, and meetings.
*   **Asynchronous Communication:** As a globally distributed team, we prioritize clear documentation and asynchronous updates over unscheduled meetings.

---

## 2. Remote Work & Core Hours

### 2.1 Workspace & Ergonomics
Employees working remotely are expected to maintain a secure, quiet workspace with a reliable high-speed internet connection. Acme Corp provides a **$500 home office stipend** upon onboarding to assist with desk, chair, or monitor setups.

### 2.2 Core Hours
Our team spans multiple time zones. To facilitate collaboration, we observe core collaboration hours:
*   **Core Hours:** 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM EST (Eastern Standard Time)
*   All employees are expected to be online and reachable via Slack during these core hours, unless on pre-approved leave.

---

## 3. Leave & Time-Off Policies

### 3.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)
*   **Annual Allowance:** Full-time employees receive **25 days of Paid Time Off** per calendar year, accrued monthly.
*   **Unused PTO Carryover:** A maximum of 5 unused PTO days can carry over into the next calendar year. Any additional unused days expire on December 31st.
*   **Request Notice:** PTO requests of 3 days or more must be submitted at least **2 weeks in advance** through the HR portal.

### 3.2 Sick Leave
*   **Allowance:** Employees receive **10 fully paid sick days** per year.
*   **Notification:** If you are unable to work due to illness, please notify your team lead and log your absence in the HR portal by 9:00 AM local time.
*   **Medical Documentation:** A doctor's note is required for consecutive sick leave exceeding **3 business days**.

### 3.3 Parental Leave
*   Acme Corp offers **16 weeks of fully paid parental leave** to all new parents (birth, adoption, or foster placement) who have been with the company for at least 6 months.

---

## 4. Travel & Expense Reimbursement

### 4.1 Business Travel
All business-related travel must be pre-approved by your department head.
*   **Flights:** Economy class for flights under 6 hours; Premium Economy or Business class may be approved for flights exceeding 6 hours.
*   **Lodging:** Hotel stays should not exceed **$250 per night** (excluding taxes) unless traveling to high-cost metropolitan areas (e.g., San Francisco, New York, London), where the limit is **$350 per night**.

### 4.2 Meals & Incidentals
*   **Daily Allowance (Per Diem):** Acme Corp reimburses up to **$80 per day** for meals and incidentals during business travel. Receipts are required for all individual expenses over $25.
*   **Client Entertainment:** Client dinners or entertainment expenses must be documented with the names of attendees and the business purpose.

### 4.3 Subscriptions & Software Stipend
*   Employees may expense up to **$50 per month** for professional subscriptions, books, or productivity software directly relevant to their roles, subject to manager approval.

---

## 5. Information Security & Data Privacy

### 5.1 Device Security
*   All company-related work must be conducted on secure, authorized hardware.
*   **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** MFA must be enabled on all corporate accounts (Google Workspace, Slack, GitHub, Railway, etc.).
*   **Passwords:** Password sharing is strictly prohibited. Use the company-provided password manager for sharing system credentials.

### 5.2 Customer Data Confidentiality
*   Never share customer Personal Identifiable Information (PII) or proprietary codebase secrets over unsecured channels.
*   API keys, DB connection strings, and encryption keys must never be committed to Git repositories in plain text.

---

## 6. AI Assistants & Escalation Guidelines

Acme Corp utilizes AI Employee Bots (e.g., Alison for HR, Alex for Support) to automate routing, help answer general queries, and organize work.

### 6.1 Interacting with AI Bots
*   Our bots (like ALEX) are trained on this handbook and internal document uploads.
*   To ask a bot a question in a Slack channel, mention them directly (e.g., `@Alex how many PTO days do I get?`).
*   To converse privately, open a Direct Message (DM) thread with the bot.

### 6.2 Escalation Process
*   If an AI assistant encounters a request that requires human validation, it will trigger an **Escalation Request**.
*   A manager will receive a request to Approve or Deny the action (e.g., approving a special refund, modifying system configuration, or signing off on a policy exception).
*   Once approved or denied by the manager, the bot will resume and post the resolution back to the original Slack thread.
